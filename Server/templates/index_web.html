QUCoughScope




Welcome to the QUCoughScope, developed by Qatar University.

QUCoughScope uses artificial intelligence to infer probability of COVID-19 infection from cough and breath sound along with several other information. Our deep learning models are trained on large public datasets. The sensitivities of asymptomatic and symptomatic patient detection are 95.86% and 92.77%, respectively.
